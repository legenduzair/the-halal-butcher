About Us...

Calling All Meat Lovers!

The Halal Butcher was initiated in May 2022 with the sole purpose of supplying our meat-loving audience with the very best cuts that is to offer.


Our whole ethos is built upon delivering the very best cuts of Halal meat, from prime beef steaks to fresh chicken meat to authentic sushi! Regardless of what type of meat we are selling, we will not offer any compromise in either the process or quality of our product.


Our animals are all free-range and grass-fed which we feel is important for their happiness. This reflects in the meats as the quality is extraodinary and rich in flavour once prepared appropriately to your liking.


Our product is 100% halal and our livestock is slaughtered unstunned fulfilling all sharia requirements.
